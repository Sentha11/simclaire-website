Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you stay connected while traveling.

An eSIM is a digital SIM that lets you activate a mobile plan without a physical SIM card.
Activation occurs when you arrive at your destination or when you enable the eSIM, depending on the plan.
Most modern iPhones, Samsung, and Google Pixel devices support eSIM. Contact support if unsure.
