Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SimClaire eSIMs

An eSIM is a digital SIM that allows you to activate a mobile plan without inserting a physical SIM card.

Your eSIM activates when you arrive at your destination or when you enable it, depending on the plan.

Most modern smartphones support eSIM. Check your device settings or contact support.
