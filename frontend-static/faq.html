Getting Started

An eSIM is a digital SIM that lets you activate a mobile plan without inserting a physical SIM card.

Your eSIM activates when you arrive at your destination or when you enable it in your phone settings.

Compatibility & Devices

Most modern smartphones support eSIM. Your device must be eSIM-compatible and unlocked.
