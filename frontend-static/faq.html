Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you stay connected while traveling.

An eSIM is a digital SIM that lets you activate a mobile plan without inserting a physical SIM card.

Your eSIM activates when you arrive at your destination or when you enable it, depending on the plan.

Most modern iPhones, Samsung, and Google Pixel devices support eSIM.
